Quaestiones Frequentes
Frequently Asked
What time should we arrive at the venue? +
We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before the ceremony begins to give you enough time to find your seat and settle in. The ceremony will start promptly, so please do not be late!
Is there parking available at the venue? +
Yes, parking is available near the ceremony area. Our ushers will guide you to the designated parking spaces upon arrival.
Are children allowed at the event? +
We love your little ones, but due to the outdoor setting and space limitations, the ceremony is for adults only. Children are most welcome to join at the reception afterward.
